Privacy Policy

We keep this short and readable. This page explains what we collect, why we collect it, and how you can control your information on drop.cv.

What we collect

We collect the information you choose to upload or enter, such as your name, resume content, profile links, and account details. We also collect basic usage data to help us improve the product and keep the service secure.

How we use it

We use your information to create and host your public profile, provide analytics, support your account, and deliver product updates. We do not sell your personal data.

Sharing

We only share information when it is necessary to operate the service, comply with the law, or respond to your requests. Public profile content is visible to visitors when you choose to publish it.

Your choices

  • You can update or delete your profile content from your account.
  • You can hide your page or disable public discovery visibility.
  • You can contact us if you need a copy of your data or want it removed.

Contact

If you have a privacy question, reach out through the support contact in your account dashboard.
