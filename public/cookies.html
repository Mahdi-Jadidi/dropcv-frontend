Cookie Policy

This page explains the cookies and browser storage we use, and why they matter to your account and draft data.

Authentication

We set the dropcv_token cookie when you sign in or create an account. It keeps your session active and is marked httpOnly so the browser can send it to the server without exposing it to page scripts.

Device storage

We store dropcv_lang in local storage to remember your language choice and dropCV_deployment to remember the current draft state in the dashboard.

Analytics and third parties

We do not use advertising cookies. Some pages load third-party services for fonts, payments, or optional integrations, and those services may set their own cookies or receive technical data according to their own policies.

Managing preferences

You can clear cookies and site data from your browser settings at any time. If you block cookies entirely, sign-in will not work because the service depends on the auth cookie.

Contact

If you have a question about cookies or browser storage, contact us or email support@drop.cv.
