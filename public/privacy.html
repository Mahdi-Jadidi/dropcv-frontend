Privacy Policy

We keep this short and readable. This page explains what we collect, why we collect it, and how you can control your information on drop.cv.

What we collect

We collect the information you choose to upload or enter, such as your name, resume content, profile links, and account details. We also collect basic usage data to help us improve the product and keep the service secure.

When you sign in, we set an authentication cookie so your session stays active. We also store a language preference and draft state in your browser so the interface can remember where you left off.

How we use it

We use your information to create and host your public profile, keep you signed in, provide analytics, support your account, send account or billing emails, and deliver product updates. We do not sell your personal data.

Cookies and device storage

Our Cookie Policy explains the specific cookies and local storage items we use, including the dropcv_token authentication cookie and the dropcv_lang and dropCV_deployment browser storage keys.

Sharing

We only share information when it is necessary to operate the service, comply with the law, or respond to your requests. Public profile content is visible to visitors when you choose to publish it. Service providers that help us run the product, such as hosting, email, analytics, storage, and payment partners, may receive the minimum information required to perform their work.

Your choices

  • You can update or delete your profile content from your account.
  • You can hide your page or keep it private until you are ready to publish.
  • You can clear cookies and browser storage at any time from your browser settings.
  • You can contact us if you need a copy of your data or want it removed.

Contact

If you have a privacy question, reach out through our contact page or email support@drop.cv.
